C. HEXADECIMAL
You’ve always find it difficult to divide Hexadecimal numbers, right?
Dividing Hexadecimal is not that difficult, actually it’s the most complicated
Thing to do manually. That’s why it is recommended to do the following steps:
Step 1:
Convert the dividend and the divisor into its
decimal equivalent.
Step 2:
After converting, divide the numbers in decimal way.
Step 3:
Convert the quotient and remainder (if any) inti its
hexadecimal equivalent and that gives you the final
answer.
It is really advisable to convert hexadecimal to decimal rather that binary and use
binary division because of the following reasons.
  Decimal is our everyday system of arithmetic.
  Binary equivalent of Hexadecimal is too long, dealing with these
voluminous set of numbers is prone to commit mistakes.
